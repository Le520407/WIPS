Privacy Policy

Ace Star Tech System Integration Pte Ltd

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

Quick Summary: This Privacy Policy explains how Ace Star Tech collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our WhatsApp Business Platform and Embedded Signup services. We comply with Meta's Platform Terms, Singapore's PDPA, and international data protection standards.

1.Introduction

Ace Star Tech System Integration Pte Ltd ("Ace Star Tech", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our:

  • WhatsApp Business Platform and Embedded Signup services
  • Website (wa.acestartechsi.com)
  • API integration services for Odoo and other platforms
  • WhatsApp Cloud API connectivity solutions

By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2.Information We Collect

2.1 Business Information

We collect the following types of data necessary to provide our services:

  • Company Details: Business name, registration number, industry, contact details
  • WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) Information: WABA ID, Phone Number ID, display name
  • Account Credentials: API tokens, access tokens (stored encrypted)
  • Integration Configuration: Webhook URLs, verify tokens, Odoo instance details

2.2 Message Metadata

For service delivery and logging purposes, we process:

  • Message timestamps and delivery status
  • Message IDs and conversation IDs
  • Sender and recipient phone numbers
  • Template names and parameters
  • Webhook event data

Important: We do not collect or store the actual content of your WhatsApp messages beyond what is necessary for logging and technical support. Message content remains between you and your customers.

2.3 Technical and Log Data

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP addresses and access logs
  • Error logs and performance metrics
  • Session data and authentication records

3.How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide Services: Enable WhatsApp messaging, manage your WABA connection, and deliver API integration
  • Embedded Signup: Facilitate Meta's Embedded Signup flow for connecting your WhatsApp Business Account
  • Authentication: Verify your account and manage secure access
  • Message Delivery: Send, receive, and log WhatsApp messages through the Cloud API
  • Support: Respond to inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide technical assistance
  • Compliance: Meet Meta's Platform Terms, Developer Policies, and legal obligations
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance service quality and performance

We will never:

  • Sell, rent, or lease your personal data to third parties
  • Use your message content for advertising or marketing
  • Share your data without your consent (except as required by law)

4.Meta Data Handling (WhatsApp Cloud API)

As a Meta Tech Provider, we process WhatsApp Business Account data through Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API.

4.1 Data Shared with Meta

We share the following with Meta Platforms for service delivery:

  • WABA IDs and Phone Number IDs
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status, message IDs)
  • Template configurations and webhook subscriptions
  • System user assignments and permissions

4.2 Compliance with Meta's Terms

This data exchange complies fully with:

  • Meta's Platform Terms
  • Meta's Developer Policies
  • WhatsApp Cloud API Terms
  • Applicable privacy and data protection laws

5.User Consent

By using our services, you provide explicit consent for Ace Star Tech to:

  • Process and store your business data securely
  • Share necessary information with Meta Platforms for message delivery and account verification
  • Maintain logs and audit records as required by Meta's Tech Provider program
  • Integrate your WhatsApp account with third-party platforms (e.g., Odoo) as per your configuration

You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us (see Section 12). However, this may limit or terminate your access to our services.

6.Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or comply with legal requirements:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Logs and Audit Records: Retained for up to 90 days for security and compliance
  • Billing Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Singapore law
  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted or anonymized 30 days after account termination

6.1 Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@acestartechsi.com. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion in writing.

7.Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All access tokens and sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256, TLS 1.3)
  • Access Control: Role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Audit Logs: All data access logged and reviewed regularly
  • Compliance: Adherence to Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and international standards

8.Data Subject Rights

Under Singapore's PDPA and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

If Meta or a regulatory authority notifies us of a user's data rights request, we will promptly inform the relevant business client as required under Meta's Tech Provider obligations.

9.Incident Notification

In the event of a privacy or data security incident, we will:

  1. Notify Meta Platforms and affected clients as soon as practicable (within 72 hours)
  2. Take immediate corrective action to mitigate the issue
  3. Conduct a thorough investigation and provide a detailed incident report
  4. Fully cooperate with regulatory investigations if required
  5. Implement additional safeguards to prevent future incidents

10.Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers for:

  • Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud Platform, or similar
  • Database: MongoDB Atlas (encrypted storage)
  • Integration: Odoo, CRM systems
  • Analytics: Application performance monitoring (anonymized)
  • Communication: Email delivery, SMS notifications

Each provider is contractually bound to protect your data and may only process it for authorized purposes. We conduct regular vendor audits to ensure compliance.

11.International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in locations outside Singapore (e.g., AWS Singapore, Google Cloud Asia-Pacific). When transferring data internationally, we ensure compliance with:

  • Singapore's PDPA transfer requirements
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Appropriate safeguards as required by law

12.Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal obligations, or Meta's requirements.

The latest version will always be available at: https://wa.acestartechsi.com/privacy

Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
